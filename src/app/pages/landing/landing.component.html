CAD Marine is a maritime consulting firm based in Panama, specializing in ship broking and marine services. About Us

Ethics, precision, and a global perspective.

We are a team of maritime professionals driven by reliability, clarity, and strategic insight. From our headquarters in Panama, one of the world’s most vital maritime hubs, we provide brokerage, bunkering, and advisory services that empower our clients to make smarter, faster, and safer decisions.

Mission

To deliver maritime brokerage and consultancy services that generate real value for our clients through trustworthy, efficient, and transparent processes, leveraging Panama’s strategic position as a global logistics center.

Vision

To become a global benchmark in ship broking and marine services, recognized for our ethics, trust, and capacity to deliver effective solutions in a changing industry.

Purpose

To be the most reliable strategic partner in the maritime market, connecting shipowners, operators, and clients with complete transparency and efficiency.

Services

At CAD Marine, we integrate the essential pillars of maritime commerce:
  • Ship broking and chartering
  • Maritime consultancy
  • Market analysis and advisory
  • Vessel operations support
  • Strategic connections between shipowners, operators, and clients
  • Bunkering and logistics
